Part 1: Generate App-Specific Password
1

2

3

4

5
Click the + button (or Generate Password)
6
Enter a label: "Spot Funnel"
7
Click Create
8
Copy the 16-character password (format: xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx)
